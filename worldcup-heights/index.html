How it was measured

Roster from the official 2026 World Cup squads (Wikipedia). Heights joined from Wikidata by stable ID, with Wikipedia infoboxes and Transfermarkt filling the gaps — then the outliers cross-checked by hand. Built once into a static file — no live calls.

The honest caveats

Height: all 1,248 players covered — 1,143 from Wikidata, the rest from Wikipedia infoboxes and Transfermarkt. 44 implausible or conflicting values were checked by hand and corrected against Transfermarkt, FotMob and Wikipedia.
Heights are self-/club-reported and rounded to the centimetre. Weight sat out this study — too sparse to trust.
